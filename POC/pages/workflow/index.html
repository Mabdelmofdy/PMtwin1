Platform workflow

End-to-end flow from registration to contract execution

PMTwin connects professionals, consultants, and companies through a clear lifecycle: register, get vetted, discover opportunities, apply, negotiate, and contract.

Matching

Application and negotiation

Applicants apply to opportunities; owners review, shortlist, and negotiate. Status flow: pending → reviewing → shortlisted → in_negotiation → accepted or rejected.

Pipeline
7

Contract

Accepted applications become contracts (pending → active → completed or terminated).

Contracts
8

Execution and closure

Parties deliver on scope and milestones; contracts are completed or terminated; opportunities close.
